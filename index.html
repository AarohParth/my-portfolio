About Me

I am Aaroh Parth Asthana, a B.Tech Mechanical Engineering student (2021-2025) from MMMUT, Gorakhpur. My academic journey has provided me with a strong foundation in core mechanical engineering principles, complemented by practical experience in design, simulation, and manufacturing.

My passion lies in 3-D Designing and Modelling, and advanced simulations using tools like Fusion 360, ANSYS SpceClaim, ANSYS Fluent, LS-DYNA, Explicit Dynamics, Static Structural & Multiphysics. I have gained hands-on experience through internships at the Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO) and Hindalco Industries Ltd., where I contributed to projects involving cryocooler design, CFD analysis, and manufacturing operations.

Beyond academics and professional pursuits, I have a keen interest in Simulations, 3-D Printing, Automation in Machines, Rocket Propulsion Systems, and Renewable Energy Systems. I am dedicated to continuous learning and applying engineering knowledge to create impactful and efficient solutions.
